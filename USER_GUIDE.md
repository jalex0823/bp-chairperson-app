## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Registration

**Q: How do I get a registration key?**  
A: Email backporchmeetings@outlook.com with "Request BP Chair Key" in the subject line.

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**  
A: Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page and follow the email instructions.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**  
A: Yes, update it in your Profile settings.

### Chairing Meetings

**Q: Can I chair multiple meetings?**  
A: Yes! You can sign up for as many meetings as you're comfortable with.

**Q: What if I'm unavailable last minute?**  
A: Withdraw from the meeting as soon as possible using the Withdraw button on your Profile page.

**Q: How do I know what type of meeting it is?**  
A: The meeting detail page shows the description (e.g., "AA-approved literature only").

**Q: What are gender-restricted meetings?**  
A: Some meetings are for men only or women only. The app automatically filters these based on your profile.

### Technical

**Q: Can I view the calendar without logging in?**  
A: Yes! The calendar and meeting information are public. You only need to login to sign up for meetings.

**Q: Does the app work offline?**  
A: After your first visit, basic functionality works offline. New data requires internet connection.

**Q: What browsers are supported?**  
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions recommended).

**Q: Can I export meetings to my personal calendar?**  
A: Yes! Use the "Download Day (.ics)" or "Download Month (.ics)" buttons on meeting detail pages.

### Help & Support

**Q: I found a bug. Who do I contact?**  
A: Email backporchmeetings@outlook.com with details about the issue.

**Q: Can I suggest a new feature?**  
A: Absolutely! Send suggestions to backporchmeetings@outlook.com.
