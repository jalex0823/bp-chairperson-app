How to Register as a Back Porch Chairperson

Before You Begin

To register as a Back Porch chairperson, you should have:

  • At least 90 days of continuous sobriety (suggested minimum)
  • A sponsor and be working the steps (suggested)
  • Regular attendance at Back Porch meetings
  • A valid registration unlock key from the admin team

Registration Process

Step 1: Get Your Unlock Key

Contact a Back Porch admin to receive your registration unlock key. You'll need this to access the registration form.

Admin Contact: Available through regular meeting channels

Step 2: Complete Registration

Fill out the registration form with your information:

  • Display name (first name + last initial)
  • Email address
  • Secure password
  • Gender (for meeting eligibility)
  • Sobriety date (optional)

What to Expect After Registration

  • View the monthly calendar showing all Back Porch meetings
  • Click on dates to volunteer for chairperson duties
  • Sign up for existing meetings that need chairs
  • Express availability for dates without scheduled meetings

  • View all your meeting commitments with dates, times, and Zoom links
  • Track your service statistics and volunteer history
  • Access chairperson guidelines and meeting resources
  • Manage your availability and preferences

  • Confirmation emails when you volunteer for dates
  • Reminder emails 24 hours before meetings you're chairing
  • Meeting details including Zoom links and guidelines
  • Administrative updates about schedule changes
