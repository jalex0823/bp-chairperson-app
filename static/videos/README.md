# Training Videos

Place your training video files in this folder with these exact names:

## Required Files:
- `registration_tutorial.mp4` - How to Register to Host a BP Online Zoom Meeting
- `hosting_tutorial.mp4` - How to Host a Zoom Meeting

## Format:
- MP4 format (H.264 video, AAC audio)
- 720p or 1080p resolution
- 16:9 aspect ratio

## Note:
These videos are displayed in the Training Quizzes section of the application.
Users must watch at least 80% of each video before they can submit the quiz.
